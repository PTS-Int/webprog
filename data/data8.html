9. Line Tracking Car

Project Name: Line Tracking Car
Subject: 2014S1 - Hardware Synthesis Lab
Details: This is the final project of Hardware Synthesis Lab. Students are assigned to work as pairs and create a "line tracking car" using Microprocessor. Every group has to build a car themselves and write a program in C language. The car will use the sensor to track the blackline and moves along the line until the end of track.
Role: Car designer, Tester
